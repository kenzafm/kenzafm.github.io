F1 Tire Time
Simulation

Simulating the fastest tire time based on user's lap and tire choices.

Synopsis

In a Formula One race, the team’s choice of tires and when to change them can greatly affect the race results. The tire strategy is about picking which tires to use and deciding the best time to make a pit stop. F1 teams use complex computer simulations to plan these strategies. Here, we’ll look at a simpler version of this problem.
