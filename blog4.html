Least Squares
Synthesis

In this project, we will be walking through everything that makes Least Squares Linear Regression one of the most popular and overused models today.

Synopsis

The core idea of linear regression is that it finds the best-fitting straight line through a set of data points. I’ll be taking an intuitive approach and constantly showing visuals throughout to best understand the following concepts, since math is always best understood when visualized!
