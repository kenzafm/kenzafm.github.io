Data Advocacy
Committee

In my Digital Literacy Class, we had to use academic concepts of rhetoric to convince others on our mision as a committee-like group. Our given topic was Data Privacy.

Synopsis

The project is to demonstrate understanding and practice of rhetoric in different writing forms of genres, mediums and modes. The goal is to learn about these various writing structures academically. Expected deliverables include an argumentative essay, blog posts, podcast, website and many more!
